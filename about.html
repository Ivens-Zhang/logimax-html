Company Introduction

The company is committed to becoming the largest wine import and export company in China, as well as a wine brand management and operation company.

Professional Founder

ONE 9&9 Pty Ltd was founded in Adelaide by Chinese founders who currently specialize in MBA in wine business at the University of Adelaide

Business Support

Our company is one of the memberships of ThincLab, which is the business incubator of the University of Adelaide, and has our own independent office in ThincLab

Professional Team

We have a professional media team, an internet big data analysis and AI team, a sales team (China), and a brand management and operations team (China)

Our subsidiary

Chinese Subsidiary

Nanjing Chao Le Chuang Jie International Trading Co., Ltd., the Chinese subsidiary of ONE 9&9 Pty Ltd, specializes in the sales of Australian wines as an importer and responsible for wine brand management and operation in China. Currently, we have established a reliable network of 300 Chinese agents. At the same time, we are continuously developing more Chinese agents.

We plan to act as an agent for 10-50 boutique wine brands from various regions in Australia and provide brand management and operational services for 100-200 Australian wine brands, catering to the diverse cooperation needs of wineries and B-customers in China. (Wineries are welcome to send brand information, product details, and FOB prices through contact us)

Team Advantages

Our media production team, big data analysis and AI team, and China brand management and operations team all have many years of industry experiences. With deep insights into trends in the Chinese wine market and a precise understanding of Chinese customer needs, we continuously innovate and optimize to help wineries accurately penetrate the Chinese market, enhance brand awareness and market share, and stand out in the competitive Chinese market, achieving long-term sustainable development.

Future Prospect

In the future, we will work with the University of Adelaide’s Wine Business School, Wine Australia, and the SA Government to jointly promote the digital transformation and upgrading of the Australian wine industry, making an important contribution to the digital development and export of the Australian wine industry.
